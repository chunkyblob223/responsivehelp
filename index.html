As we enter our thirty-first exciting season, all of us here would like to thank all of those who have helped make us one of the most popular summer programs in Southern California. We at California Junior Lifeguard Programs are dedicated to providing our participants with the best instruction in ocean safety, lifeguarding skills, and surfing instruction. Our junior lifeguard instructors are professionals in the areas of ocean safety and related activities. Our surfing instructors are world-class surfers and have extensive experience in instruction for all surfing ability levels. So please enjoy our site and remember SAFETY FIRST.
